Blending Modes
A feature unique to digital illustration.
Blending Modes are unique to digital illustration that change how layers of color interact, one layer can darken the image based on its color, or make it lighter and glow, or even change the color entirely.

Post-Editing
Make overall changes after painting.
Post editing is useful for making paintings pop more in digital illustration, you can add filters, exaggerate contrast and saturation, or even change color. Here I used it to dramaticize the contrast and add a grainy effect.
